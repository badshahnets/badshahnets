Why Bale Net?

Plastic mesh Bale Net is quickly becoming an attractive alternative to twine for the wrapping of round hay bales. The soft white netting has many advantages when compared to twine. Which include:

  • Reduced time to wrap and full width wrapping.

  • Improved quality of hay and smooth lay-flat bale surface.

  • Easy removal and End of Roll Marking.
